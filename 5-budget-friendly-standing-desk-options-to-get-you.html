The Benefits of Standing Desks

Before we dive into our top picks, let's quickly explore why standing desks are worth considering in the first place. By switching to a standing desk, you can:

  • Improve your posture and reduce back pain

Manual Crank Desks: Are They Right for You?

Manual crank desks may seem old-fashioned compared to electric sit-stand desks, but they have their own set of benefits.

  • Cost-effective: Manual crank desks are significantly cheaper than their electric counterparts.
  • Low maintenance: With fewer moving parts, manual crank desks require less upkeep and repair.
  • Improved posture: The physical effort required to adjust the height can help improve your posture and engage your core muscles.

However, manual crank desks may not be ideal for everyone.

  • More effort required: Switching between sitting and standing requires more physical exertion than electric sit-stand desks.
  • Limited adjustments: Manual crank desks often have limited adjustment options compared to their electric counterparts.
